# about the PosterSettigns.txt file

Fill each line with a syntax. Market will post based on these settings with the ruleset as follows.

Items must be in your characters inventory to the left as they autosort(columns of 5 height). Assumed 1x1 slot items for now.

## syntax

`count, price`

## samples

`10, 199`   

post 10 items at 199 gold each
